About R Pooja Bangles Store

Welcome to R Pooja Bangles Store, where tradition meets elegance. We specialize in authentic Meenakari bangles, kadas, and bracelets that showcase the rich heritage of Indian craftsmanship.

Our collection features handcrafted pieces with intricate enamel work, vibrant colors, and timeless designs. Each bangle tells a story of skilled artisanship passed down through generations.

From everyday wear to special occasions and bridal collections, we offer a wide range of designs in various colors and patterns. Our meenakari work features traditional floral motifs, geometric patterns, and contemporary designs that appeal to modern tastes while honoring ancient traditions.

Our Specialties: Traditional Meenakari Bangles, Kundan Work, Bridal Sets, Custom Designs, Bulk Orders for Weddings & Events
